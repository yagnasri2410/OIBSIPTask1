Course we offer

University of Oxford offers a wide range of courses.

Intermediate

Intermediate courses refers to courses that are taken at a level between undergraduate and postgraduate studies.However, Oxford University does offer a variety of short courses, workshops, and summer programs that can be considered intermediate or continuing education. These are often designed for professionals, adult learners, or individuals looking to enhance their skills or knowledge in a specific area.

Under Graduation

Bachelor of Arts (BA) in English Language and Literature,Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Physics,Bachelor of Arts (BA) in History,Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBChB),Bachelor of Philosophy (BPhil) in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics (PPE),Bachelor of Laws (LLB),Bachelor of Engineering (BEng) in various engineering fields,Bachelor of Music (BMus).Oxford offers many more undergraduate programs.

Post Graduation

Postgraduate courses includes master's and doctoral programs,in various fields of study.Master of Arts(MA) in Archaeology,Master of Business Adminstration(MBA),Master of Science (MSc) in Environmental Change and Management,Doctor of Philosophy (DPhil) in Physics,Master of Public Policy (MPP),Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science,Master of Theology (MTh) in Theology and Religion
